DataViz Palettes

Ever fallen into the trap of picking out a color palette for your data visualization work only to realise later that your choice colours doesn't quite hit the mark on your graph. This tool lets get a sense of what your palette would look like on a variety of charts, while also allowing you quickly make changes and see how they play out.

You can also quickly import colors from your favourite color palette generators and experiment with what they would look like in your viz.
